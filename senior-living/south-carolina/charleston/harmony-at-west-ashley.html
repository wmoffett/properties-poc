Harmony at West Ashley is designed to put you in control of your future, knowing that the finest care is available to you – all on one private campus. The continuum of care at West Ashley includes Independent Living, Assisted Living, and Harmony Square, the Memory Care Neighborhood of our community.

Harmony at West Ashley is a convenient, comfortable and active community of understated elegance. It is a place where you choose the lifestyle you prefer, while you and your family have the peace of mind that comes from planning for the future.

At Harmony at West Ashley, many of life’s chores are taken care of, giving you back time and energy to pursue your interests and enjoy your independence.
